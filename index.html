About

Who I am and how I work

I blend hands-on data analytics with a solid understanding of IT systems. I enjoy digging into real-world datasets, finding the signal in the noise, and then designing dashboards and processes that match how people actually work.

Background

I’ve worked across analytics and IT support: building reports and trackers while also dealing with imaging, warranty workflows, and Windows 10/11 rollouts. That means I understand both the numbers in the dashboard and the systems behind them.

How I think

I like to keep things simple: start with a messy spreadsheet or dataset, clean it up, create a clear view with the right metrics, and then automate as much as possible so insights are always up to date.

What I’m looking for

Roles where I can combine data analysis, business problem-solving, and system thinking — especially as a Data Analyst or Business Analyst, with the bonus of understanding technical operations.
