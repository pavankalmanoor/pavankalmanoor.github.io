Background
Who I am

I'm a Data Scientist with 2+ years of experience building production ML systems across healthcare, SaaS, NLP, and transit. At UMass Boston I've built LLM-powered pipelines, predictive models, and data infrastructure — processing 8,000+ records weekly and cutting triage time by ~60%. Before that, I built real-time dashboards for the Government of India's national distribution system at the National Informatics Center.

How I think

I start with the business question, not the data. From there I find the right tool — a SQL query, a scikit-learn classifier, a DSPy-orchestrated LLM pipeline, or a Docker-containerised deployment — and build something reproducible, interpretable, and actually used by the people who need it.
