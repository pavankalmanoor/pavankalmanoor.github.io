Background
Who I am

I'm an ML Engineer & Data Analyst at the University of Massachusetts Boston, where I build predictive models, LLM-powered automation workflows, and scalable data pipelines — processing 8,000+ asset records weekly and cutting manual triage time by ~60%. Before that I worked as a data analyst at the National Informatics Center in India, building real-time dashboards for the Government of India's national distribution system.

Approach
How I think

I start with the business question, not the data. From there I find the right tool — a SQL query, a scikit-learn classifier, a DSPy-orchestrated LLM pipeline, or a Docker-containerised deployment — and build something reproducible, interpretable, and actually used by the people who need it.
